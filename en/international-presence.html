International presence

Balms Advisory is a company that acts on a level with the support of the global BGI network (BALMS Group International), present in 24 countries and on four continents, through offices in major economic centres, such as London, Paris, Munich, Milan and Bucharest in Europe, Mexico DF and Lima in South America, Tangier in Africa or Beijing in China.

This international presence follows our motto Think globally act locally, which allows us to have a 365º vision of the market situation in which our Client wants to grow or to introduce itself, as well as a realistic and effective local knowledge of the market situation of the destination of the investment.

Any need of our Client in another country is immediately taken care of locally by our professionals in said geographical Region, giving the Client a tranquility both for the immediacy and, above all, for the knowledge of said market and its particularities.
