Strategic consulting


Balms Advisory is a business consulting firm that offers integral solutions to the Client, aimed at creating value, adapting to the demands of the market and the Clients, managing the digital transformation to be able to sell more through the new sales channels, delimit the risk and increase the profitability and competitiveness of our Clients.


Market analysis

The objective is to study the commercial viability of a project for the Client, be it a new product in its usual market, or a geographically new market for the Client. To do this, it is necessary to determine the geographic scope, quantify the potential market, group the market into homogeneous groups or segments with the same client profile, divide the segments into niches or subgroups, select the one in which they want to position themselves and calculate their potential demand and of sales. The motivations and buying behavior of the clients and their needs will be analyzed. Finally, a prospect is made about the future evolution expected of the market and the impact that new technologies may have on it.

We detail the characteristics of the market (legal, technical, logistics, marketing, production, organizational aspects), its structure, such as barriers to entry, the main geographical areas (countries, regions, islands, municipalities, neighborhoods), the size to be efficient and achieve an adequate return on investment and profitability (number of total customers, degree of concentration or dispersion of such clients in the region), as well as forecasts and growth trends in demand.

Marketing and Communication

Strategic marketing allows knowing where a company is and where it wants to go. An essential strategic analysis so that a company can, not only survive in its market, but position itself in a preferential place and be able to face the constant challenges that are presented in the evolution of the business activity.

We help our clients to position themselves in the market in an increasingly competitive way, projecting the image they want to have of their company. The marketing tools that are used for this are:

  • Marketing strategies

  • Corporate Marketing

Digital transformation

Digital transformation is the new business strategy opportunities that arise thanks to the emergence of technologies. Likewise, this change is not only technological, but brings with it new aptitudes both in physical persons, as well as in the reinvention of organizations that affect the traditional global market. It is not focused solely on the technology used (Big Data, cloud, mobility, social business) but on using it to achieve the objectives set. The digital transformation will be linked with the business objectives and strategies.

The digital transformation reconstructs the dynamics of organizations to adapt them to the needs of the present and the future. It is a challenge that involves the entire organization of a company, not just the technology part, since a considerable part of the sale, planning, logistics, production or provision, review and billing of the product or service can already be done through digital applications that force us to adapt business structures to the new reality. Clients demand it and the competition is already working in that direction, so also from BALMS Advisory we offer this service so that Clients can transform their structures and organizations in step with the demand and expectations of their markets.

Mergers and acquisitions (M&A) and post-merger integration

We help companies to manage risk in corporate restructurings such as mergers, spin-offs, contributions of branch of activity, acquisitions, etc. Increasing your profitability and creating value.

We work closely with our clients to help them manage their business risks, improve the performance of their operations and increase the opportunities of their transactions, especially in the following areas:

  • Acquisition strategy
  • Search for financing
  • Due diligence
  • Legal and fiscal assistance in restructuring
  • Post-acquisition assistance in financial and operational areas
  • Strategic alliances
  • Establishment abroad

The integration processes are key to meet the objectives set, achieve benefits, maintain the prestige of the company and so that there is an acceptable level of sales. To achieve the objectives set in the negotiation phase, it is necessary to develop an action plan that includes a smooth transition, executive leadership support, participation in the management of the two affected parties and everything in a context of constant and multidirectional communication during the integration process.

Post-merger integration (PMI) involves the transfer of large volumes of knowledge, the assimilation of systems and the alignment of goals and milestones.
