Investment consultancy


Balms Advisory is an investment consultancy and for all types of projects and organizations, advising on the part of business strategy as well as on the part of operations and processes, as well as everything related to the digitization of your organization and the sale through the different channels in apps, internet and e-commerce that are currently prevailing in all businesses and sectors.

BALMS Advisory also advises clients looking for projects for their investments in different sectors that may be of interest to them. Our BALMS Advisory Team works in the process of buying and selling companies, advising Private Equity funds and investment funds, as well as family offices.

We support you in the development of business plans and strategic advice of your Organization from the moment you want to make a market analysis to enter a new business area or a new geographical territory. After this analysis of the market, we elaborate with you the business plan so that your company develops the activity with full confidence and guarantee of success.

Balms Advisory analyses the financial decisions of any company in terms of return on investment and annual EBIT, the benefit you will receive with that investment. These investment decisions require structured analytical support to the Client and must take into account the cash flows of the projects, but also their risks, both market and geopolitical and strategic. The BALMS Advisory Professional Team includes the necessary instruments to analyze all these elements and make your investment successful from the beginning of the project.

In a world where business opportunities are constantly evolving, Balms Advisory provides a general perspective and a range of new business possibilities that can attract customers across countries. Whether the client is looking for a reliable project to invest in, or if, on the other hand, they have a project for which they need private financing, Balms Advisory offers a wide range of possibilities and offers in various geographical locations.
